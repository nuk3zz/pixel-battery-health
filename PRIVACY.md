# Privacy

Pixel Battery Health is designed to run fully offline.

- The app does not request internet permission.
- The app does not upload bugreports.
- The app reads only the bugreport ZIP selected or shared by the user.
- Shared bugreports may be copied to Downloads so the user keeps a local copy.
- Extracted working files are stored in app cache.

Android bugreports may contain sensitive diagnostic logs. Keep generated bugreport files private unless you intentionally choose to share them.
